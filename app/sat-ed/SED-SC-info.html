Goals

  • Increase provider capacity for evidence-based treatment and recovery support services to youth with substance use disorders or substance use with co-occurring mental illness
  • Improve health outcomes among youth with substance use disorders or substance use with co-occurring mental illness
  • Enhance use of finances and resources through coordination of funding streams, treatment, and recovery support services for adolescents and families to facilitate program expansion and sustainability.

Population Served

Youth (ages 12−17) in York, Orangeburg, Bamberg, and Calhoun counties
