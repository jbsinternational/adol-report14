Goals

  • Improve access to a dual diagnosis capable providers for youth in the population of focus.
  • Develop and improve access to quality treatment for adolescents with substance use and co-occurring mental health and substance use disorders and their families.
  • Develop a strategic plan for identifying additional providers to be trained in ACRA post grant funding
  • Develop funding strategies that support the infrastructure needed to sustain the EBPs at the end of CSAT funding

Population Served

Adolecents (ages 12−18) with juvenile justice involvement
