Goals

  • Develop two learning laboratories in two sites that are centrally located and will meet the needs of community and State.
  • Establish infrastructure for training and adoption of the Comprehensive Adolescent Severity Inventory (CASI) assessment tool and Multidimensional Family Therapy (MDFT) in youth-serving MCP’s throughout Indiana.
  • Utilize the Government Performance and Results Act (GPRA) to improve outcomes for participating children and adolescents, as measured by National Outcome Measures (NOMs)
  • Accomplish changes in local and statewide policies and practices that facilitate implementation of CASI and MDFT.
