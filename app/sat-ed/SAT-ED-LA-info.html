Goals

  • Develop and improve access to quality assessment and treatment for adolescents with substance use and co-occurring mental disorders and their families through the implementation of an evidence-based treatment model and an evidence-based assessment that will enable a feedback loop for collaborating agencies to: (1) engage in process improvement; (2) identify and resolve gaps in services; and (3) measure client outcomes in real time.
  • Ensure that family, youth, and community stakeholders have an impact on the expansion of the program during years 02 and 03.
  • Ensure that the evidence-based practices will be sustained at the end of the CSAT funding.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) with substance abuse/co-occurring disorders and their families
