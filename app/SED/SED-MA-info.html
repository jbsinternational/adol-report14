Goals

  • Promote a comprehensive, integrated, high-quality service system for youth with substance use and/or co-occurring problems.
  • Demonstrate the effectiveness of enhanced and improved service quality and recovery support to adolescents with co-occurring disorders and families in the community.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) with substance use and co-occurring substance use and mental health disorders
