Goals

  • Develop and improve access to quality treatment for adolescents with substance use disorder and co-occurring substance use disorder and mental health disorder diagnosis and their families/caregivers.
  • Improve health outcomes for the 300 adolescents involved in this project.
  • Support and include family and adolescent voice at the policy and practice levels.
  • Develop and/or enhance a coordinated network of treatment and recovery support services for youth with substance use disorder and co-occurring substance use disorder and mental health disorder diagnosis and their families/caregivers throughout the State.
  • Expand capacity of the workforce in both the specialty sector and other child-serving agencies.

Population Served

Youth (ages 12–17) and their families
