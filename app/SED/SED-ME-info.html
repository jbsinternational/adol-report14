Goals

  • Implement the Adolescent Community Reinforcement Approach (A-CRA) with 680 youth (ages 12−18) with substance abuse or substance abuse and co-occurring mental health disorders, and their families.
  • Expand evidenced-based adolescent substance abuse and/or co-occurring treatment services across the State.
  • Expand the corps of a qualified culturally competent adolescent treatment workforce.
  • Develop practical and sustainable funding/payment strategies.

Population Served

Youth (ages 12−18) and their families
