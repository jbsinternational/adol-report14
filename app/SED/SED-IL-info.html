Goals

  • Enhance the Illinois statewide infrastructure in support of a continuum of services for adolescents with substance use disorders in order to (1) strengthen the voice of youth and their family members in the implementation of these services and (2) improve the quality of care and supports provided.
  • Enhance the clinical services available to adolescents with substance use disorders in Illinois through the statewide implementation of evidence-based assessment, treatment, and recovery support practices.
  • Document the results of Illinois SAT-ED infrastructure and clinical service enhancements; obtain evidence of positive outcomes among the adolescents who are admitted to the clinical treatment services supported through this cooperative agreement.

Population Served

Majority: Hispanic/Latino(a) male and female youth (ages 12−18 years)
