Goals

  • Increase access of adolescents (ages 12−18 ) with substance abuse/mental health or co-occurring disorders to evidence-based treatment services.
  • Improve the quality of treatment and recovery services through workforce capacity building on recognized EHB in both the public and private treatment systems of Puerto Rico through a partnership of the Mental Health and Anti-Addiction Services Administration (MHAASA) with two local CBO providers.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18 ) with substance abuse/mental health or co-occurring disorders
