Goals

  • Enhance the capacity of the Chickasaw Nation by increasing access to and improving the quality of care provided to youth, transitional-aged youth, and their families.
  • Enhance the effectiveness and improve the quality of care for substance use and co-occurring disorder services provided to youth, transitional-aged youth, and their families.
  • Select two evidence-based practices to be implemented in two tribal provider sites.

Population Served

Adolescents and transitional-aged youth (ages 12−24) and their families/primary caregivers
