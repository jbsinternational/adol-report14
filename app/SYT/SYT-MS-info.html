Goals

  • Build capacity and sustain infrastructures at state and local levels that meet the complex needs of adolescents with substance use/other co-occurring disorders, and their families.
  • Increase/improve accessibility and effectiveness of culturally appropriate treatment and recovery services for adolescents with substance use and co-occurring disorders and for their families.
  • Conduct a rigorous evaluation to ensure compliance with all data collection and reporting requirements, and assess the effectiveness of the pilots for replication.
  • Complete all required grant management activities effectively and within required timeframes.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) and their families
