Goals

  • Develop and improve access to quality treatment for District of Columbia adolescents with substance use and co-occurring mental disorders and their families.
  • Train and certify clinical staff and clinical supervisors in the Adolescent Community Reinforcement Approach (A-CRA) and the Global Appraisal of Individual Needs (GAIN) family of instruments.
  • Monitor and evaluate A-CRA implementation.
  • Sustain and enhance co-occurring services for clients and families.
  • Certify additional programs in the delivery of the A-CRA model.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) during Year 1; both adolescent (ages 12−18) and transitional-aged youth (ages 18−24) in Year 3 and Year 4, using Medicaid and local funding to implement the evidence-based practice
