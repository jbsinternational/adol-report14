Goals

  • Identify and prepare community-based provider agencies to implement Adolescent Community Reinforcement Approach (A-CRA) with transitional-aged youth (TAY) with substance use disorders (SUDs)/co-occurring diagnoses and their families/caregivers.
  • Implement A-CRA with TAY and their families/caregivers in Regions 2 and 4.
  • Track outcomes to inform process; analyze data and use results for system improvements.
  • Increase evidence-based treatment services to TAY with SUDs or SUDs/mental health diagnoses and their families/caregivers through system improvements.
  • Develop financing structures to continue and expand use of A-CRA services with TAY and their families/caregivers after grant funding ends.

Population Served

Transitional-aged youth (ages 18−24) and their families
