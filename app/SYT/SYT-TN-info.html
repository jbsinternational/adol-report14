Goals

  • Partner with two community-based treatment provider sites to provide comprehensive treatment and recovery support services that encompass the complexities of addiction, mental health (including trauma), and recovery.
  • Develop the infrastructure and capacity to increase access to and improve quality of services for adolescents and transitional-aged youth and their families.
  • Improve health status and outcomes for participants.
  • Develop/disseminate a thoroughly documented service model for replication/adoption across the State.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) and transitional-aged youth (18−24) and their families
