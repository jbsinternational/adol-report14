Goals

  • Establish, implement, and fully utilize a State-level partnership in order to guide, support, and sustain statewide systems change efforts.
  • Expand, at the community level, the evidence-based treatment service options for youth (ages 12−24) in order to more effectively respond to this population's needs.
  • Expand both the referrals to the youth treatment system and the total number of youth services by Vermont Alcohol and Drug Abuse Program’s preferred/approved provider group in order to more completely respond to the needs of this population (ages 12−17 and 18−24).
  • Evaluate and document the implementation of this project in order to fully inform future system enhancement efforts at the community, statewide, and national levels.

Population Served

Adolescents (ages 12−18) and transitional-aged youth (18−24) and their families
